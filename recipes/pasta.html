Pasta

Description

This is another recipe that is great for many people to make. It's full of protein and carbs, making it a nice meal to eat after a workout or after doing some work! Since it's pasta, it's also a nice meal to make for others and flex your newfound cooking skills.

I recommend that when reheating this pasta, add a little bit of heavy cream before microwaving so it tastes just as good as when you frist made it.

Ingredients

  • Tomato and Basil Pasta Sauce
  • Oregano
  • Heavy Cream
  • Pasta Noodles (~ 8 oz)
    • It can be any kind of pasta
  • Sriracha Sauce
    • Only use this if you want your pasta to be spicy
  • Vegetables
    • Can be something like carrots, broccoli, cauliflower, etc.
  • Meat
    • Either chicken or italian sausage
  • Mozzarella Cheese

~5-6 servings

Steps

  1. Get a pot and boil some water for about 10 minutes
  2. Take add your pasta to the boiling water and leave it for 7-10 minutes until pasta is soft
  3. Add some salt and mix it with the pasta
  4. When pasta is done cooking, pour all the water out and let pasta sit
  5. Get a pan and let it warm up for a few minutes
  6. Once the pan is hot, cook your meat and then cook your vegetables
  7. Set aside the meat and veggies, and then pour the tomato sauce into the pan
  8. Then, pour the heavy cream into the pan and make sure to mix
    • If you want more spice, add the sriracha sauce
  9. Sprinkle a handful of mozzarella cheese
  10. Once this new mixture starts to get more gooey, put your pasta, meat, and vegetables into the pan and mix
  11. enjoy >:)
