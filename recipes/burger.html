Burgers

Description

Need something quick for July 4th, need to host a party, or just plain hungry but don't feel like eating out? This burger recipe is not only easy to make, but also delcious and healthier than most fast food places!

fun fact: it's healthier to eat two burgers over a burger and fries

Ingredients

  • 6 Beef Patties
  • One tomato
  • Half an onion
  • Lettuce
  • American Cheese Slices
  • Burger Buns

~3-4 servings

Steps

  1. Cut the tomatos into slices
  2. Cut the onions into cubes
  3. Cut the lettuce into slices
  4. Take a pan, and set it on medium heat
  5. Take your onions, and cook them until they are really warm and/or a caramel color
  6. Set aside your onions, and cook your patties
  7. Make sure to flip them, cooking them until both sides of the patty are brown, with the middle being a dark pink color
  8. Set the heat to low, and add your cheese on the patties
    • If possible, cover the pan so the cheese melts faster
  9. Now, set aside your patties, and take out your burger buns, and cook them on the pan until they're a golden color
  10. Take your ingredients, and now build them into a burger!
  11. Of course, enjoy :)))
